Research Overview

This comprehensive study in General Insurance Techniques covers advanced actuarial methodologies, statistical modeling, and practical applications in the insurance industry. The research focuses on developing robust analytical frameworks for risk assessment, pricing, and reserving in general insurance operations.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Advanced risk modeling and statistical analysis techniques
  • Comprehensive understanding of loss distribution fitting methods
  • Proficiency in collective risk theory and compound distributions
  • Expertise in ruin theory and capital adequacy assessment
